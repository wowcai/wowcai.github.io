## Data And Method Notes

- This report uses target-level forecast summaries from the four final runs and does not rerun the simulation.
- Final picks keep concrete names, while evidence sections reduce proper nouns and exact values.
- Import coverage is complete across all four runs, with zero errors.
- Prediction status is consistent across the runs, with a small matching synthetic-fill scope.
- Some trace fields are not fully consistent across runs, so they are not treated as core consensus evidence.
- Target-level posterior values are relative candidate-pool strengths, not direct real-world probabilities.
- Top-four synthesis first identifies the candidate pool, then selects the best full placement combination.
- Award synthesis first uses winner votes; ties use average strength and contextual consistency.
- The evidence is a display-oriented summary, not a line-by-line reproduction of raw evidence cards.
- Conclusions should be read as long-range forecasts, with split items preserved through broad risk wording.
